What does VaR mean in simple terms?
Value at Risk (VaR) is the worst loss you'd expect on a normal day, with a given confidence level. Reliance's 99% VaR is −3.84%, which means:
  • On 99 out of 100 typical trading days, losses should stay within 3.84%.
  • Roughly 1 day in 100, the loss could be larger than that.
Think of it as a "speed limit" for everyday downside — not a worst-case guarantee.
